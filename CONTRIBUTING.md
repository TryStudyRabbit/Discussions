# 🤝 Contributing to StudyRabbit Discussions

Thanks for your interest in contributing to the **StudyRabbit Community**!  
We welcome your ideas, questions, and feedback to make StudyRabbit the ultimate productivity tool for students and creators.

---

## 🗂️ Where to Post

| Category | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| 💬 Q&A | Ask questions or seek help from the community. |
| 🚀 Feature Ideas | Suggest new functionality for StudyRabbit. |
| 🐞 Feedback & Bugs | Report bugs or provide general feedback. |
| 🎨 UI/UX Suggestions | Share ideas about layout, design, or user flows. |
| 🤝 Collaborations | Looking to team up or contribute? Let us know here. |

---

## ✅ Guidelines

- Be respectful and kind to others.
- Use clear titles and provide context.
- One idea/issue per post for clarity.
- Upvote discussions you support!
